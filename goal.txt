  ---
  Why this order makes sense

  - Each phase validates before scaling — no point training 128M if the pipeline has issues
  - 50M is cheap enough to experiment on MPS, large enough to show meaningful results
  - Instruction tuning data is small — Phase 3 is fast (~few hours)
  - By Phase 4 you understand every moving part
